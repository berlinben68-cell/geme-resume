# ExecGit Pricing Strategy

**Strategy:** Purchasing Power Parity (PPP) based pricing to maximize penetration in India while capturing high value in the UAE.

## India Pricing (Focus: Job Seekers & Aspirants)
**Goal:** Volume & Accessibility.

### 1. The "Aspirant" Plan
*   **Price:** ₹999 / month (~$12)
*   **Target:** Junior/Mid-level Devs wanting to look busy.
*   **Features:**
    *   5 Ghostwritten Commits / week.
    *   Basic "Green Square" Scheduler.
    *   Standard Code Styles.

### 2. The "Tech Lead" Plan
*   **Price:** ₹2,499 / month (~$30)
*   **Target:** Senior Devs & Architects.
*   **Features:**
    *   Unlimited Commits.
    *   **Style Matcher (Code DNA)**.
    *   LinkedIn Post Generator (Basic).

---

## UAE Pricing (Focus: Executive Branding)
**Goal:** Premium Service & High Touch.

### 1. The "Executive" Plan
*   **Price:** AED 499 / month (~$135)
*   **Target:** CTOs & Founders.
*   **Features:**
    *   **Audio-to-Repo Engine** (Voice Note Support).
    *   **NDA-Safe Sanitizer** (Proprietary Code Upload).
    *   **Trend Surfer** (Auto-suggestions).
    *   Priority Support.

### 2. The "Thought Leader" Plan
*   **Price:** AED 999 / month (~$270)
*   **Target:** High-Profile Tech Leaders.
*   **Features:**
    *   Everything in Executive.
    *   **Viral LinkedIn Content Strategy** (Auto-posting).
    *   **Personalized RAG Memory** (Learns your specific coding history).
    *   Dedicated Account Manager.
